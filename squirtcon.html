Squirtle is a water type Pokemon introduced in Gen 1. It evolves into Wartortle, which evolves into Blastoise. Squirtle is one of three starter Pokemon available at the beginning of Pokemon Red, Green, Blue, FireRed and LeafGreen.

Wartortle is a water type Pokmon Intoduced in Gen 1. It evolves from squirtle and evolves into Blastoise. Wartortle is a bipedal, indigo Pokemon similair to a turtle. A brown shell with a pale yellow underside encases its body.

Blastoise is a water type Pokemon introduced in Gen 1. It evolves from Wartortle which comes from Squirtle. Blastoise is the game mascot of the Japanese and international versions of Pokémon Blue, appearing on the boxart of both.
