Zapdos is an electric flying Pokemon introduced in Gen 1, it's one of the three legendary birds of Kanto. This Pokemon usually appears during thunderstorms and gets its power when it is struck by lightning. When airborne it’s wings create crackling/snapping sounds as well as releasing electricity causing thunderstorms. Zapdos’s first ability is pressure, it’s 2 hidden abilities are static and lightning rod. It is not known to evolve into any other Pokemon.
