Charmander is a fire type Pokemon intoduced in Gen 1. It evolves into Charmeleon, which evolves into Charizard. Along with Bulbasaur and Squirtle, Charmander is on of three starter Pokemon at the beggining of Pokemon Red, Green, Blue, Firered and LeafGreen.

Charmeleon is a fire type Pokemon inroduced in Gen 1. It evolves from Charmander and evolves into Chariard. Charmeleon is a bipeedal, reptillian Pokemon. It has dark red scales and a cream underside from the chest down.

Charizard is a dual type fire and flying Pokemon introduced in Gen 1. It evolves from Charmeleon, which evolves from Charmander. Charizard is the game mascot of Pokemon Red and Firered versions.
