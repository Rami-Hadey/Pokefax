Electric Type Water Type Fire Type Psychic Type Normal Type Ghost/Poison Type

Lickitung is a normal type Pokemon that has a round body and a thick,
powerful tail. It is pink with a long tongue. The tongue is both dexterous
and powerful enough to lift people. Abilities include: Own Tempo and oblivious.

Lickilicky is the evolvolved form of lickitung, through it's evolution it almost doubles in size.
